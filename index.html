Our Shared Learning Index

Welcome to Critical Fields, a decentralized, peer-to-peer platform for early-career thinkers engaged in interdisciplinary critical studies. Our goal is to make the intellectual ecosystems of critical scholarship more visible, accessible, and open-source. We gather blogs, reading lists, events, calls for submissions, and networks across the disciplines of economics, anthropology, cultural studies, history and politics. We also spotlight work that addresses inequality, post-coloniality, and the specific philosophical and pragmatic questions facing our generation. Critical Fields platforms independent contributions to our shared learning index. Join our network and consider sharing to the open educational commons.
